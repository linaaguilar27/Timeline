Join the PCI Working Group to revise FSC’s Principles, Criteria, and Indicators

FSC is revising its core forest management standards – Principles & Criteria (P&C), International Generic Indicators (IGI), and related normative documents (collectively the PCI). To support this global revision process, FSC is pleased to announce the reopening of applications for roles to support the PCI Working Group (WG) and invites expressions of interest for the following roles.

Open positions
  • One expert with strong experience in natural forest management
  • One expert with strong expertise in Biodiversity and High Conservation Value (HCV)
  • Researcher (observer role)

Application period: 12 February – 26 February 2026.

This two-week reopening will include targeted outreach via regional offices and network partners.

How to apply

Refer to the draft Terms of Reference for detailed information on roles and responsibilities of the PCI Working Group.

Please submit your expression of interest by Thursday, 26 February 2026 to the Forest Management team at forestmanagement@fsc.org. Indicate the position in the email subject line, specify in the email body your FSC chamber affiliation (if applicable), relevant areas of expertise, and gender. Applicants should attach their CV and include a cover letter outlining their motivation for applying and their suitability for the role.

Important: Applicants who have already applied do not need to resubmit their application unless they wish to be considered for one of the newly advertised positions (natural forest management or biodiversity/HCV expert). Please note that the expert seats are open to both FSC members and non-members.

Do you know someone who would be a great fit? Please share this call or apply here.

Public Consultations on PCI Revision

FSC launched a consultation on the revision of its Principles and Criteria, International Generic Indicators, and other forest management normative documents (collectively referred to as PCI). The consultation closed on December 31, 2025.

Call for Working Group and Consultative Forums to revise FSC’s Principles, Criteria and Indicators (closed)

Between 15 October and 30 November 2025, FSC invited interested candidates to apply to join the Working Group and the Consultative Forums responsible for revising the Principles and Criteria, the International Generic Indicators, and other Forest Management documents.

During this period, FSC also published the Draft Terms of Reference, which outlined the scope of work and composition of the Working Group.
