October 1 – December 31, 2025 ›
Public Consultations on PCI Revision

FSC launches consultations on the revision of its Principles and Criteria, International Generic Indicators, and other forest management normative documents (collectively referred to as PCI). The consultation is open from 01 October 2025 until 31 December 2025.

Consultation is available in English, Spanish, and French.

Further details: Consultation Content, Consultation Overview, Consultation Guideline.

Call for Working Group and Consultative Forums to revise FSC’s Principles, Criteria and Indicators

Between 15 October and 30 November 2025, FSC invited interested candidates to apply to join the Working Group and the Consultative Forums responsible for revising the Principles and Criteria, the International Generic Indicators, and other Forest Management documents.

During this period, FSC also published the Draft Terms of Reference, which outlined the scope of work and composition of the Working Group. The Working Group held decision-making responsibilities and was supported by experts, liaisons, a facilitator, a youth representative, FSC staff, and the Consultative Forums.
