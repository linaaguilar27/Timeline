Public Consultations on PCI Revision

FSC launches consultations on the revision of its Principles and Criteria, International Generic Indicators, and other forest management normative documents (PCI). The consultation is open from 01 October 2025 until 31 December 2025.

Consultation is available in English, Spanish, French.

Further details: Consultation Content, Consultation Overview, Consultation Guideline.

Call for Working Group and Consultative Forums

Between 15 October and 30 November 2025, FSC invited candidates to apply for the Working Group and the Consultative Forums responsible for revising FSC’s Principles and Criteria and International Generic Indicators.

FSC also published the Draft Terms of Reference, outlining the scope and composition of the Working Group, made up of experts, liaisons, a facilitator, a youth representative, FSC staff, and members of the consultative fora.
