15 October – 30 November 2025 ›
Call for Working Group & Consultative Forums

Between 15 October and 30 November 2025, FSC invited interested candidates to apply to join the Working Group and the Consultative Forums responsible for revising:

  • FSC Principles and Criteria
  • International Generic Indicators (IGI)
  • Other Forest Management normative documents

During this period, FSC also published the Draft Terms of Reference, which outlined the scope of work and composition of the Working Group.

The Working Group holds decision-making responsibilities and is supported by experts, liaisons, a facilitator, a youth representative, FSC staff, and the Consultative Forums.

October 1 – December 31, 2025 ›
Public Consultations on PCI Revision

FSC launches consultations on the revision of its Principles and Criteria, International Generic Indicators, and other forest management normative documents (collectively referred to as PCI).

The consultation is open from 01 October 2025 until 31 December 2025.

Consultation is available in English, Spanish, and French on the FSC Consultation Platform. Please refer to the Consultation Content, Consultation Overview, and Consultation Guideline for more details.
